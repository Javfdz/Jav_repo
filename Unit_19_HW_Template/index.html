Javaier Fernandez Flota

I am a UX UI designer and have 10 years in the beauty and banking industries. I enjoy looking at things such as an app and imagining opportunities to be improved upon and work proactively to solve problems. As a stylist and banker I have developed empathy as a skill set which allows me to understand other people's pain points easily. My Ideal client is someone who is hands on and open to new ideas and opinions.

My UX Skills

User Research

I use tools like user interviews, surveys, redlining and annotations when applicable to learn user needs and wants. Thus providing me the foundation to move to the next steps in the project.

User Empathy

With the data obtained during user research I create an empathy map which helps obtain user insights as to what their experiences are like and what their desires may be.

Qualitative interviews

I use interviews to find information on how people often perform certain tasks and why or why not. In addition to find information on where specifically focus my attention to.
